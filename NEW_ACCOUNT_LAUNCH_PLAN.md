### "1 Video + 1 Sentence" Templates

#### Template 1: Price Shock
**Hook:** "$42 for this" (2 sec - show location exterior)
**Body:** [3 sec - best view/moment footage, no talking]
**End Card:** "Worth it?" with Yes/No buttons (2 sec)

**Virality Triggers:**
- Price in first frame = instant curiosity
- Binary question = comment bait ("Yes!" / "Overpriced!")
- Relatable to anyone planning travel

### Engagement Pod Strategy

**What is an Engagement Pod?**
Group of 10-25 Instagram accounts that agree to like/comment on each other's posts within 30 minutes of posting to boost algorithm performance.

**How to Join:**
1. Search Discord servers for "engagement pod" or "support group" channels
2. Look for travel-niche pods (avoid generic pods with fashion/fitness mixed in)
3. Apply with your handle, follower count, niche, posting schedule
4. Most pods require 100+ followers (you'll qualify by Week 2)

**Pod Rules (Standard):**
- Like + meaningful comment on every member's post within 30 min
- Post 3-4x per week (matches your schedule)
- Share posts in pod channel immediately after posting
- No ghosting (if you miss 3 posts, you're removed)

**Expected Results:**
- 15-20 guaranteed likes per reel (from pod members)
- 10-15 comments per reel
- Instagram sees early engagement = pushes reel harder to Explore page
- Converts to 2-3x more non-follower views

**When to Join:** Week 2 (once you hit 100-120 followers from Week 1 organic growth)

## Risks & Mitigations

### Risk 1: Discord Servers Ban Promo
**Mitigation:** Don't drop links unsolicited. Only share reels when asked for feedback in dedicated feedback channels. Build relationships first, promote second.

### Risk 2: Engagement Pods Get Shadowbanned
**Mitigation:** Join travel-niche pods only (Instagram can detect fake engagement from unrelated accounts). Vary comment text (no copy-paste). Space out likes (not all within 2 minutes).

### Risk 3: Viral Replication Looks Stolen
**Mitigation:** Copy structure, not content. Use YOUR footage, YOUR locations, YOUR captions. Only replicate hook formula, duration, and CTA style.

### Risk 4: Burnout from 4 Reels/Week
**Mitigation:** Batch create on Sundays (90 min session = 4 reels). Schedule via Meta Business Suite. Each reel is 5-8 seconds = low production burden.

### Risk 5: Content Library Runs Out
**Mitigation:** You have 15-20 locations across 6 countries. At 4 reels/week, that's 4-5 weeks of unique content. By Week 5, remix old locations with new formulas ("I was wrong about Louvre" = revisit with opposite take).
