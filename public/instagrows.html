    Growth Tips & Best Practices

    üéØ Low Skip Rate Formula

    1. Hook (0-1 second)

    • Use pattern interrupt: "Wait", "Stop", "POV", "Things nobody tells you"
    • Show best visual first OR biggest text
    • Create curiosity gap

    2. Pacing (Every 2-3 seconds)

    • Cut to new clip/angle
    • Change text overlay
    • Movement in every frame (zoom, pan, motion)

    3. Payoff (Last 3 seconds)

    • Deliver on the promise
    • Ask a question (drives comments)
    • Loop potential (last frame connects to first)

    4. Optimal Length

    • Sweet spot: 12-15 seconds
    • Avoid: 30+ seconds (retention drops)

    5. Text Overlays

    • Large, bold, readable on mobile
    • Change text every 2-3 seconds
    • Use emojis sparingly

    6. Audio Strategy

    • Trending audio = 2x reach
    • Check "Audio" tab in Reels for trending sounds
    • Avoid copyrighted music (gets muted)

    üí¨ Engagement Tactics (Authentic, Not Spammy)

    Comments (Most Important):

    • Reply to every comment within first hour of posting
    • Ask follow-up questions to keep conversation going
    • Pin best comment to top (boosts engagement)
    • Never use generic replies ("Thanks!")

    Stories:

    • Post 3-5 stories per day
    • Use interactive stickers: Polls, Questions, Quizzes
    • Repost user content (builds community)

    • Avoid over-1M hashtags (too competitive)

    • Tag every landmark/city featured
