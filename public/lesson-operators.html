What are Operators?

Operators are symbols that perform operations on variables and values. Python has several types of operators.

Arithmetic Operators:

Operator Description Example
+ Addition 5 + 3 = 8
- Subtraction 5 - 3 = 2
* Multiplication 5 * 3 = 15
/ Division 6 / 3 = 2.0
** Exponent 2 ** 3 = 8

Comparison Operators:

  • == Equal to
  • != Not equal to
  • > Greater than
  • < Less than
