üé§ Duo Presentation Tips for Success

üë• Collaborate as Partners

  • Meet with your partner to plan your presentation flow
  • Decide who presents which features (2-3 min each)
  • Create ONE cohesive slide deck together
  • Practice smooth transitions between speakers
  • Support each other during Q&A
  • Divide work equally and fairly

üìä Prepare Your Content

  • Each person researches their specific section deeply
  • Practice using each tool multiple times
  • Create examples BEFORE presenting
  • Have a backup plan if technology fails
  • Share a single Word document to demonstrate

üéØ Structure Your Duo Presentation

  • Intro (20 sec): Both partners introduce your ribbon tab
  • Partner 1 (2-3 min): First section demo
  • Partner 2 (2-3 min): Second section demo
  • Conclusion (20 sec): Quick summary & Q&A
  • Total Time: 4-6 minutes

üíª Live Demonstration

  • Open Word and show features in REAL-TIME
  • Narrate what you're doing as you do it
  • Each person builds on the previous person's work
  • Zoom in so everyone can see
  • Have a prepared document ready to go

üó£Ô∏è Delivery Tips

  • Speak clearly and at a good pace
  • Make eye contact with the class
  • Explain WHY each feature is useful
  • Stay within 4-6 minutes total
  • Smooth handoff between partners
  • Both partners should be engaged the whole time

‚ú® Make It Flow

  • Show how your features work TOGETHER
  • Build one document progressively (Partner 1 starts, Partner 2 continues)
  • Tell a story with your presentation
  • Connect features to real-world uses
  • Divide work evenly (each partner 2-3 min)
