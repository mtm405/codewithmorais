üé§ Group Presentation Tips for Success

ÔøΩ Collaborate as a Team

  • Meet as a group to plan your presentation flow
  • Decide who presents which section (2-3 min each)
  • Create ONE cohesive slide deck together
  • Practice transitions between speakers
  • Support each other during Q&A

üìä Prepare Your Content

  • Each person researches their specific section deeply
  • Practice using each tool multiple times
  • Create examples BEFORE presenting
  • Have a backup plan if technology fails
  • Share a single Word document to demonstrate

üéØ Structure Your Group Presentation

  • Intro (30 sec): Overview of your group's tab(s)
  • Person 1 (2-3 min): First section demo
  • Person 2 (2-3 min): Second section demo
  • Person 3 (2-3 min): Third section demo
  • Person 4 (2-3 min): Fourth section demo
  • Conclusion (30 sec): Summary & Q&A

üíª Live Demonstration

  • Open Word and show features in REAL-TIME
  • Narrate what you're doing as you do it
  • Each person builds on the previous person's work
  • Zoom in so everyone can see

üó£Ô∏è Delivery Tips

  • Speak clearly and at a good pace
  • Make eye contact with the class
  • Explain WHY each feature is useful
  • Stay within 8-12 minutes TOTAL as a group
  • Smooth handoffs between group members

‚ú® Make It Flow

  • Show how your sections work TOGETHER
  • Build one document progressively
  • Example: Format text ‚Üí Insert image ‚Üí Add header ‚Üí Review
  • Tell a story with your presentation
